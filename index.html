What is Yalanehgez

Booking automation
built for Egypt.

A B2B SaaS platform that connects your WhatsApp Business number to an AI booking assistant — automating every step from first message to confirmed reservation.

Egyptian businesses lose bookings every day to unanswered messages, slow replies, and staff overwhelmed during peak hours. Yalanehgez eliminates the bottleneck — your AI assistant is always online, always polite, and never misses a reservation.

We integrate directly with your existing WhatsApp Business number. Bookings are logged automatically to Google Sheets or your preferred management system. No new app for your customers to download. No complex system for your staff to learn.

Yalanehgez is built on the WhatsApp Cloud API (Meta) and powered by Claude AI (Anthropic), enabling natural, accurate conversations in both Arabic and English.

Privacy Policy

Your data,
protected.

Last Updated: May 2025

1. Introduction

Yalanehgez ("we", "us", or "our") operates a WhatsApp-based booking automation platform for businesses in Egypt. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data that is processed through our platform in connection with the WhatsApp Business API, provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.

By using or interacting with a business powered by Yalanehgez via WhatsApp, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you are a business operator using our platform ("Client"), this policy also governs your obligations with respect to the personal data of your end customers.

2. Data We Collect

When an end customer interacts with a Yalanehgez-powered business via WhatsApp, the following data may be collected and processed as part of the booking workflow:

  • Name — provided voluntarily by the customer during the booking conversation
  • Phone number — the WhatsApp number used to initiate contact
  • Booking details — date, time, party size, service type, and any special requests relevant to the reservation
  • Message content — the text of WhatsApp messages exchanged during the booking process, to the extent necessary to process the reservation

We do not collect payment card details, national ID numbers, health records, biometric data, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to facilitate a booking transaction.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected through the Yalanehgez platform is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and confirming reservations on behalf of the business you are booking with
  • Sending booking confirmations and pre-visit reminders via WhatsApp
  • Maintaining booking records for the venue or business operator
  • Improving platform reliability and AI conversation quality, using anonymised or non-identifiable data where applicable

We do not use your personal data for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose unrelated to completing and managing your booking.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

Your data may be shared with the following parties solely to deliver the booking service:

  • The business you are booking with — the venue or operator receives your name, phone number, and booking details in order to honour your reservation
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — as the operator of the WhatsApp Business API (WhatsApp Cloud API), Meta processes message data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Yalanehgez operates as a business solution provider using the WhatsApp Cloud API on behalf of its clients
  • Anthropic, PBC — the AI that powers the booking conversation is provided by Anthropic. Message content may be processed by Anthropic's systems to generate AI responses, subject to Anthropic's usage policies and privacy standards
  • Google LLC — booking records may be stored in Google Sheets for venue record-keeping purposes, subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

All third-party providers maintain their own privacy policies and data protection obligations. We select providers that meet reasonable standards for data security and compliance.

5. WhatsApp Business API Compliance

Yalanehgez operates through the WhatsApp Cloud API in full compliance with Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy, WhatsApp Business Terms of Service, and applicable messaging guidelines.

We do not initiate unsolicited outbound messages to end users. All automated messages sent through the platform are either (a) direct responses to a user-initiated conversation, or (b) transactional messages — such as booking confirmations or reminders — that the user has opted into by beginning the booking process.

Yalanehgez does not use the WhatsApp Business API for bulk marketing campaigns, unsolicited promotional messages, spam, or any messaging category prohibited by Meta's policies.

6. Data Retention

Booking records — including name, phone number, and reservation details — are retained for a maximum of 12 months from the date of the booking, after which they are deleted or anonymised unless a longer retention period is required by the business operator for legitimate operational or legal reasons.

WhatsApp message content is not permanently stored by Yalanehgez beyond what is necessary to complete the booking transaction. The retention of message metadata by Meta is governed by Meta's own data retention policies.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All data transmitted between our systems is protected by HTTPS/TLS encryption. WhatsApp messages are end-to-end encrypted by Meta by default.

In the event of a data security incident that affects your personal data, we will notify affected parties as required by applicable law and take prompt steps to mitigate harm.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data processed through the Yalanehgez platform:

  • Right of Access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right of Correction — you may request that inaccurate or incomplete data be corrected
  • Right of Deletion — you may request that your personal data be deleted from our records, subject to any legal obligations to retain it
  • Right to Opt Out — you may ask to be removed from any booking-related communications associated with a specific business's booking system

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11 below. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

9. Cookies and Tracking

The Yalanehgez booking platform operates primarily through WhatsApp and does not use cookies or web tracking technologies to collect data from end customers. If you are visiting this website, standard web server logs (such as IP address and browser type) may be collected by our hosting provider for security and performance purposes. These logs are not used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

10. Children's Privacy

The Yalanehgez platform is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through our platform, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of a Yalanehgez-powered booking service after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
